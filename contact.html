Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our customer service number directly.

What information should I include in my message?

Please include your full name, contact details, and as much information about your inquiry as possible. If you're asking about a specific PG, please mention its name and location.

Can I schedule a visit to see the PG accommodations?

Yes, you can request a visit through this contact form. Please mention your preferred date and time, and we'll get back to you to confirm the appointment.

How can I report an issue with my current PG accommodation?

Current residents can report issues through this form or by calling our customer service. Please provide your room number, PG location, and a detailed description of the issue.
